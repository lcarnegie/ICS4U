The Rules:

1. 5 cards are dealt to each player (a group of 2, 3 or 4 people).

2. Before starting the game, all of the players put any pairs they happen to have in their hand down and earn a point for each pair.

3. Starting with the first player and moving clockwise, the player asks one of their opponents "Do you have a ____ ?" The card requested should be one the player has in their hand.

4. If the opponent has the card, they must give it to the player who earns a point for making a match. If the opponent does not have the card they say "Go Fish!"
The player must then pick a single card from the messed up, face down pile of cards.

5. The game is won when one person gets 10 points.
